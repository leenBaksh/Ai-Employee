## Post Content

LinkedIn automation is now running on its own — no manual posting required.

This week we hit a milestone with our AI Employee system: the Silver Tier is live and working end-to-end. Here's what that actually means in practice:

- A Gmail watcher reads incoming emails and routes them to the right queue
- A scheduler fires daily briefings and SLA checks automatically
- LinkedIn posts get drafted, queued for approval, then published via Playwright browser automation — zero manual steps after sign-off
- Every action is logged to an audit trail, and nothing goes out without human approval first

The core design principle: the AI handles the repetitive work, humans stay in control of what goes out. No auto-send. No autonomous posting. Every external action requires an approved file before it executes.

We built this as part of a hackathon challenge — proving that a small team can run like a fully-staffed operation with the right automation layer underneath.
